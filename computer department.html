COMPUTER DEPARTMENT
VISION
To be one of the best in the country by developing a motivated team of engineers, researchers, academicians and entrepreneurs in the domain.
MISSION
1.To pursue excellence in all areas of Computer Engineering and Information Technology keeping pace with the latest developments.
2.To create the ability in the students to design, develop and test world class software systems.
3.To inculcate the spirit of inquiry, team work, innovation and professionalism among the students.
4.To promote continuous learning and research in core and emerging areas.
5.To develop globally competent students with strong foundations, able to adapt to changing technology.
