Welcome to Government Polytechnic Dhule

Government Polytechnic Dhule was established in the year 1962.
The campus of the institute is spread across the 52 acres of land with academic and
administrative building, Workshop, Girls Hostels, Boys Hostels, Faculty and staff quarters,
Play Ground and Multi-Purpose Hall with Guest House.
The Institute is located at Vidyanagari, Deopur, Dhule on the Bay of Panjara River
on the north side of Dhule, 4 km from central bus stand.
In a well-planned city, the town planning of the city was designed by a notable engineer Sir
MokshagundamVisvesvaraya, awarded by Bharat Ratna.
Initially diploma course in civil engineering was introduced in 1962,
now the Institute runs five full time regular three years AICTE approved diploma courses.
