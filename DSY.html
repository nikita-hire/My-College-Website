Eligibility Criteria for Direct Second Year Engineering Admission


Documents Required:
Original + 5 set of photocopy

IMPORTANT
It is necessary for all students submit all required documents in ORIGINAL at the time of admission.
The documents will be returned to students (except LC) only after DTE/University verification process is completed.
Till then the documents will not be made available to students. Students and parents are requested to keep enough sets of attested photocopies along with them.


FOR ALL STUDENTS
  1. Admission allotment letter from DTE
  2. SSC Mark sheet
  3. HSC Mark sheet
  4. MH-CET / JEE Mains Score card
  5. Leaving or Transfer certificate
  6. micile Certificate ( or birth certificate)
  7. Nationality Certificate


WHEREVER APPLICABLE
  1. Caste Certificate
  2. Caste Validity Certificate
  3. Non - Creamy Layer Certificate (valid up to March 2017)
  4. Migration Certificate ( if HSC board is other than Maharashtra)
  5. Defence Certificate
  6. Certificate for J&K student
  7. Physical Handicapped Certificate
  8. Gap Certificate
