Students Consumer Co-Operative Stores:
In order that the student get commonly required learning
material under one roof at concession rates, co-operative store is run in the campus of the institute by the student of the institute on co-operative learns. The store is registered with the co-operative Dept. of Govt. of Maharashtra. The material like stationary, journals, files, drawings sheets, lab manuals are available in the stores. An elected body of the member with principle & three institutes on ex official president looks after the working of the stores
